Biography



Novak Djokovic was born on 22 May 1987 in Belgrade, SR Serbia, SFR Yugoslavia, to Dijana and Srdjan Djokovic. At the age of four, his parents would give him s small racket and soft foam ball, which his father stated was "the most beloved toy in his life". At the age of 6, he would be sent to a tennis camp. Not only did he attend several camps but was rapidly improving his skill. This led his father to fund Djokovic to go to different academies in the United States, Italy, and Germany.
