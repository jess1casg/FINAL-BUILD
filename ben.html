Biography



Ben Shelton was born October 9, 2002 in the United States to Bryan and Lisa Witsken Shelton, who were high ranked tennis players. He did not start playing tennis until 12 years old as he origanally had a brief football career. He rapidly gain skills and even becoming the finalest in 2020 USTA Boys 18s Singles Winter Nationals and won the 2019 USTA Boy 16s Doubles Clay Court tournament.
